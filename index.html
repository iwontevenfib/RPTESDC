TESDA - BINANGONAN

TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) Rizal Training Center Binangonan is a facility approved by TESDA that offers training programs for careers and skills, following guidelines set by both TESDA and CHED.

Get Started

Vision

The transformational leader in the technical education and skills development of the Filipino workforce.

Mission

TESDA sets direction, promulgates relevant standards, and implements programs geared towards a quality-assured and inclusive technical education and skills development and certification system.

automotive, construction, hospitality, information technology, and healthcare.

Step by Step Procedures for Enrollment

  1. 1. Choose a Course

    Decide on the vocational course you want to enroll in. You can explore the list of courses offered by TESDA Binangonan on their website or by visiting the center in person.

  2. 2. Check Prerequisites

    Make sure you meet any prerequisites for the course you’ve chosen. Some courses may require specific educational backgrounds or skills.

  3. 3. Visit the TESDA Binangonan Center

    Go to the TESDA Binangonan center in person or visit their website to obtain enrollment information.

  4. 4. Submit Requirements

    Go to the TESDA Binangonan center in person or visit their website to obtain enrollment information.

  5. 5. Fill Out Enrollment Forms

    Go to the TESDA Binangonan center in person or visit their website to obtain enrollment information.

  6. 6. Attend Orientation (if required)

    Some courses may require attendance at an orientation session before classes begin. Attend the orientation to learn more about the course structure, schedule, and expectations.

  7. 7. Receive Confirmation

    After completing the enrollment process, you will receive confirmation of your enrollment. This may be in the form of a receipt, confirmation email, or other documentation.

  8. 8. Start Classes

    After completing the enrollment process, you will receive confirmation of your enrollment. This may be in the form of a receipt, confirmation email, or other documentation.

  9. 9. Complete Course Requirements

    Fulfill all course requirements, including attendance, assignments, and assessments, to successfully complete the course.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a TESDA course in Binangonan

To enroll in a TESDA course in Binangonan, you can visit the nearest TESDA training center or satellite office. They will assist you with the enrollment process and provide information on available courses

What courses are available in TESDA Binangonan

TESDA Binangonan offers a wide range of courses in various fields such as automotive, bread and pastry, and more. You can check the official TESDA website or visit the local training center for a list of available courses.

What are the requirements for enrolling in a TESDA course?

Requirements may vary depending on the specific course you wish to enroll in. Generally, you will need to provide a valid ID, proof of educational attainment (if applicable), and other documents as specified by the training center.

Is there an age limit for TESDA courses in Binangonan

TESDA courses are open to individuals of all ages. There is no specific age limit for enrolling in most courses

How much does it cost to enroll in a TESDA course?

The cost of enrolling in a TESDA course varies depending on the program and training center. Some courses may be offered for free, while others may have minimal fees to cover materials or certification expenses.

What are the job opportunities after completing a TESDA course in Binangonan?

Completing a TESDA course in Binangonan can open up various job opportunities in industries related to your field of study. TESDA provides job placement assistance and networking opportunities to help graduates find employment.
