TESDA - BINANGONAN

TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority) Rizal Training Center Binangonan is a facility approved by TESDA that offers training programs for careers and skills, following guidelines set by both TESDA and CHED.

Mission

TVET (Technical Vocational Education and Training) Programs

These are courses designed to equip individuals with technical skills needed for specific industries such as
automotive, construction, hospitality, information technology, and healthcare.

  • This course equips students with the fundamental techniques in bread and pastry making, including dough preparation, shaping, baking, and finishing. Students learn to produce a wide range of breads, pastries, and desserts, such as bread rolls, cakes, cookies, pies, and specialty breads. Topics covered may include ingredient selection, mixing methods, fermentation, baking principles, decorating techniques, and sanitation practices.

    Bread and Pastry Production NC II

    Duration: 250 hours

    Read more

  • This course covers the principles and techniques of dressmaking, including pattern drafting, garment construction, fitting, and finishing. Students learn to interpret and modify patterns, select appropriate fabrics, and use sewing machines and other equipment effectively. Topics may include basic sewing stitches, garment assembly techniques, garment alterations, and garment embellishment. asdf asdfa sasdf

    Dressmaking NC II

    Duration: 275 hours

  • This course covers the theoretical and practical aspects of driving, including vehicle operation, traffic rules and regulations, road safety, and defensive driving techniques. Students learn to handle different types of vehicles, such as cars, trucks, and motorcycles, in various road and weather conditions. Topics may include vehicle maintenance, basic troubleshooting, and emergency procedures. Practical training typically includes on-road driving practice and maneuvering exercises.

    Driving NC II

    Duration: 118 hours

    Read more

  • Designed to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to engage in organic farming practices. This includes topics such as organic farming principles, soil and crop management, pest and disease control, composting, and organic certification processes. Participants will learn about sustainable agricultural methods that promote environmental conservation and produce healthy, chemical-free crops.

    Organic Agriculture Production NC II

    Duration: 232 hours

    Read more

  • SMAW teaches the fundamentals of arc welding using a flux-covered electrode. Students learn welding positions, techniques, and safety practices for joining metal components.

    Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW) NC I, II, and III

    Duration: 268 hours

    Read more

  • This program focuses on equipping individuals with the necessary skills to become effective trainers. The course covers topics such as adult learning principles, training needs assessment, training planning and delivery, evaluation methods, and instructional strategies. Participants will learn how to design and deliver training sessions effectively, cater to different learning styles, manage group dynamics, and assess training outcomes. This program is essential for individuals aspiring to become trainers in various fields, including vocational education, corporate training, and community development.

Step by Step Procedures for Enrollment

  1. 1. Choose a Course

    Decide on the vocational course you want to enroll in. You can explore the list of courses offered by TESDA Binangonan on their website or by visiting the center in person.

  2. 2. Check Prerequisites

    Make sure you meet any prerequisites for the course you’ve chosen. Some courses may require specific educational backgrounds or skills.

  3. 3. Visit the TESDA Binangonan Center

    Go to the TESDA Binangonan center in person or visit their website to obtain enrollment information.

  4. 4. Submit Requirements

    Go to the TESDA Binangonan center in person or visit their website to obtain enrollment information.

  5. 5. Fill Out Enrollment Forms

    Go to the TESDA Binangonan center in person or visit their website to obtain enrollment information.

  6. 6. Attend Orientation (if required)

    Some courses may require attendance at an orientation session before classes begin. Attend the orientation to learn more about the course structure, schedule, and expectations.

  7. 7. Receive Confirmation

    After completing the enrollment process, you will receive confirmation of your enrollment. This may be in the form of a receipt, confirmation email, or other documentation.

  8. 8. Start Classes

    After completing the enrollment process, you will receive confirmation of your enrollment. This may be in the form of a receipt, confirmation email, or other documentation.

  9. 9. Complete Course Requirements

    Fulfill all course requirements, including attendance, assignments, and assessments, to successfully complete the course.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a TESDA course in Binangonan

To enroll in a TESDA course in Binangonan, you can visit the nearest TESDA training center or satellite office. They will assist you with the enrollment process and provide information on available courses

What courses are available in TESDA Binangonan

TESDA Binangonan offers a wide range of courses in various fields such as automotive, bread and pastry, and more. You can check the official TESDA website or visit the local training center for a list of available courses.

What are the requirements for enrolling in a TESDA course?

Requirements may vary depending on the specific course you wish to enroll in. Generally, you will need to provide a valid ID, proof of educational attainment (if applicable), and other documents as specified by the training center.

Is there an age limit for TESDA courses in Binangonan

TESDA courses are open to individuals of all ages. There is no specific age limit for enrolling in most courses

How much does it cost to enroll in a TESDA course?

The cost of enrolling in a TESDA course varies depending on the program and training center. Some courses may be offered for free, while others may have minimal fees to cover materials or certification expenses.

What are the job opportunities after completing a TESDA course in Binangonan?

Completing a TESDA course in Binangonan can open up various job opportunities in industries related to your field of study. TESDA provides job placement assistance and networking opportunities to help graduates find employment.

About TESDA

TESDA has provided inclusive and free technical vocational education and training (TVET) through a variety of training modalities, reaching millions of our kababayans over the years.

The agency's renewed commitment to providing our kababayans with even greater access to globally competitive, high-quality tech-voc is demonstrated by the agency's new slogan and logo, which also convey to the public TESDA's goal of serving the public with a heart that is in line with the administration's "Bagong Pilipinas" brand of leadership and governance.
